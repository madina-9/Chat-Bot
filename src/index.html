Meow 🐾 I'm Felix. Ask me anything. How to make basmati rice? Rinse basmati rice until clear, soak 20–30 minutes, then cook it with a 1:1.5 rice-to-water ratio: bring to a boil, cover, simmer 10–12 minutes, and let it steam covered for 5–10 minutes before fluffing. Avoid stirring, use precise water measurement, and you’ll get long, separate, non-sticky grains every time. I also want to add cardamom. How much of it ans when do I add it? Add 2 lightly crushed green cardamom pods to the water at the very start of cooking (crack them open but don’t grind), optionally with bay leaf and cloves, and remove the whole spices before serving for a balanced, integrated aroma without overpowering the rice.
